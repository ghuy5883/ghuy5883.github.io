SPR2020 - Obstacle Course!

Platformer prototype in the fashion of athletics challenge shows like American Ninja Warrior or Wipeout.

Posted under Programming on March 24, 2020

Project Description

This was a partner project between me and Kathleen Moulton to craft a difficulty-themed game within a week and a half. Our decision was to create a fast, 5-minute demo of a game that was both comedic and challenging, drawing off of themes of an obstacle course. The resulting product is a speedrun through multiple spinning bars, hyper bouncy balls, item-hurling audience members, and rotating steps all placed to trip the player up.
